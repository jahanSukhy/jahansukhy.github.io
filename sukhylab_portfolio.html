About SukhyLab

Building bridges between technology and academia

I am Nusrath Jahan Sukhy — a self-taught web developer, LaTeX practitioner, and aspiring researcher from Sunamganj, Bangladesh. I founded SukhyLab as my personal space to build, learn, and contribute to the academic community.

When I did not get into my national university, I had two choices. Give up — or build something real. I chose to build. I taught myself web development, learned LaTeX and academic research tools, started teaching English, and launched a small food business. Every failure became a foundation.

Today, SukhyLab exists to help professors and researchers present their work beautifully online — through modern websites, organized publications, LaTeX documents, and research visualizations. All offered freely, built with precision and genuine care.

"Victory for the world — one professor website at a time."
